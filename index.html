Questions & answers

Is it really 100% offline?

Yes. Vosuba runs entirely on your Mac using local AI models (Whisper for transcription, Kokoro/Chatterbox for voiceovers). Your files are never uploaded anywhere. The only network request is optional license activation — after that, it works fully offline.

What's the difference between Pro and Studio?

Pro ($49) unlocks all Whisper models, unlimited exports, subtitle burning, speaker diarization, and all style presets. Studio ($79) adds the Voiceover Studio with 24 AI voices, voice cloning, batch processing, and the full WCAG compliance dashboard with sound detection.

Do I get free updates?

Yes. All minor updates (1.x) are free forever. Major version upgrades (2.0, 3.0) are available at a discounted upgrade price for existing customers.

Is it really WCAG 2.2 AA compliant?

Yes. Vosuba includes a built-in compliance dashboard that validates reading speed (CPS), line length, sound annotations, and speaker identification against WCAG 2.2, ADA, FCC, and DCMP standards. You can export a full JSON compliance report.

Can I get a refund?

Absolutely. We offer a 14-day no-questions-asked refund policy. If Vosuba isn't right for you, just contact us and we'll process your refund immediately.
