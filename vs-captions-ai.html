The bottom line

Use Captions.ai if…

You caption short-form video primarily on your phone and want a quick, beautiful result with minimal setup. It's the best mobile-first option for TikTok and Reels creators who aren't handling sensitive footage.

Use Vosuba if…

You work on a Mac and need a professional desktop workflow — editing longer video, handling client footage, producing WCAG-compliant captions, or captioning institutional content that cannot leave your machine.

Common questions

Is Captions.ai better than Vosuba for short videos?

For casual short-form social clips made on your phone, Captions.ai has an excellent mobile UX. For desktop production of any video — short or long — Vosuba offers more transcript control, compliance tools, voiceover, and no cloud dependency.

Does Captions.ai have a Mac desktop app?

Captions.ai is primarily a mobile app (iOS/Android) with some web access. It does not offer a native macOS desktop application. Vosuba is built exclusively as a macOS desktop app with full offline capability.

Can I use Captions.ai for WCAG or ADA compliance?

No. Captions.ai does not include WCAG validation, CPS checking, sound annotation for SDH captions, or accessibility package export. If your organisation needs to produce ADA-compliant or WCAG 2.2 AA-compliant captions, Vosuba is the appropriate tool.
