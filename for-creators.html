Common questions

Does Vosuba work without an internet connection?

Yes. Once installed, Vosuba runs entirely on your Mac. The AI transcription model (Whisper) and TTS models run locally using Apple Silicon acceleration. No internet is required for transcription, subtitle editing, or voiceover generation. An internet connection is only needed for the initial download and licence activation.

What's the difference between the tiers?

Free — unlimited transcription, basic subtitle export, WCAG validator. Pro ($49 one-time) — styled subtitles, burned-in export, batch processing, ASS export. Studio ($99 one-time) — everything in Pro plus AI voiceover studio, SDH sound labels, compliance export. See the full breakdown on the pricing page.

How accurate is the transcription?

Vosuba uses OpenAI's Whisper model. On clear audio with a native speaker, word-error rates are typically low (under 5% for English). Accuracy drops with strong accents, overlapping speakers, or noisy environments. You can edit every caption segment in the built-in editor before export.

Can I burn subtitles into a video for social media?

Yes. Vosuba uses FFmpeg to render styled subtitles directly into your video output file. The result is a single MP4 with captions permanently embedded — ready to upload to any platform without relying on that platform's caption system.
